What is Mindwise?

MindWise is our pilot app, designed with leading clinical psychologist Dr Lee Hogan (Bsc,PhD,DClinPsy), as a crisis management and self help tool for all users mental health. Upon downloading the app with or without referal, you can sign up to crisis management monitoring service CrisMan. Also it a tool which is designed to better manage everyones mental wellbeing.

MindWise is also about being MindFit. Through this we have a monthly test to gauge how your feeling currently, be it good or not so good. From this we engineer a specific plan for that month to get MindFit. This may include daily journals and goals, to guided meditations and a community of like minded people who all want to be the best version of themselves.

What is CrisMan?

From our own person experiences dealing with mental health. We understand it from the inside looking out. Partnered with a survey we found that the way we communicate on social platforms and google searches in a mental health crisis, seem to follow a trend reflecting what the person is experiencing. The tool uses a strike system and once striked out will notify your trusted people to get in touch. This tool may well be a lifeline in keeping those we love with us.
